Participated in the design and implementation of a custom single-cycle CPU using a 24-bit instruction set. Involved in instruction set design, memory organization, and I/O interface using a PS/2 keyboard and LCD display. Demonstrates deep understanding of computer architecture and low-level hardware design.

### Analog Amplifier Design (2024)
Assisted in the design and simulation of amplifier circuits using BJTs and MOSFETs with passive components. Demonstrates knowledge of analog electronics and circuit simulation.
